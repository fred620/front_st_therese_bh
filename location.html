Nearby Landmarks

Directions

From Iloilo Airport: Ride a van bound for SM City Iloilo. From there, take a jeepney or tricycle and drop off at St Therese Magdalo, Lapaz. Our boarding house is just a short walk from there.

From SM City: Take a jeepney or tricycle and ask to be dropped off at St Therese Magdalo, Lapaz. Walk for 3 minutes toward the boarding house area.

From the Transport Terminal: Take a jeepney or tricycle heading towards St Therese Mall. Once you arrive at St Therese Mall, transfer to a jeepney or tricycle going to St Therese Magdalo. The boarding house is a short walk from the mall.
